# When Search Becomes Structure

A guarded bridge note between Wolfram's ruliological P-vs-NP microcosms and tau-admissible witness construction.

This Research Note does not claim to solve orthodox P vs NP, does not identify the Ruliad with Category tau, and does not treat Wolfram's finite experiments as validation of Panta Rhei. It uses Wolfram's ruliological essay as a public comparator for the narrower question of when witness search becomes finite address construction inside a typed framework.
